Spicy Curry



Description

A bold and flavorful spicy chicken curry made with aromatic spices, coconut milk, and tender chicken. Perfect with rice or warm naan bread.


Ingredients
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp chilli powder (adjust for spice level)
  • 1 can (400 ml) coconut milk
  • 1 cup crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive olive
  • salt to taste
  • fresh cilantro

  1. heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. add onion and cook until soft and translucent.
  3. Stir in garlic and ginger, cooking for about 30 seconds.
  4. Add curry powder and chili powder, stirring to release the aroma.
  5. Add chicken pieces and cok until lightly browned.
  6. Pour in crushed tomatoes and coconut milk.
  7. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce hear and simmer for 20-25 minutes
  8. Season with salt and garnish with fresh cilantro before serving
