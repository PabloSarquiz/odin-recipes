Creamy Garlic Chicken Pasta



Description

A creamy and flavorful pasta dish with tender chicken, garlic, and parmesan sauce. Quick, satisfying, and perfect for dinner.


Ingredients
  • 2 chicken breasts (sliced)
  • 250 g pasta (penne or fettuccine)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive olive
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh parsley (optional)

Instructions
  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil and butter ina pan and cook chicken until golden.
  4. Remove chicken and saute garlic for 30 seconds.
  5. Add heavy cream and simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Stir in parmesan until melted.
  7. Return chicken to the pan and mix with sauce.
  8. Toss with cooked pasta and garnish with parsley.
